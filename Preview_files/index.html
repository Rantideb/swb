We provide comprehensive services to foster student wellbeing and empower every student to thrive during their time at Banaras Hindu University.

Student well-being services

The Student Well-Being website for Banaras Hindu University's Faculty of Arts aims to provide critical services focused on the holistic development of students.

Key offerings

The website facilitates convenient event registration, automated event management, an up-to-date event calendar, seamless certificate generation, and timely email notifications to enhance the student experience.

Student focus

By streamlining these critical services, the Student Well-Being website places students at the center and enables more enriching and fulfilling student life.
